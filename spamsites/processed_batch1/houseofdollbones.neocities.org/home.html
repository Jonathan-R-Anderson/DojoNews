Welcome to The Dollhouse!

My name is Doll Bones. Welcome! This is my personal website. A home for all my projects, content, and personal stuff. I'm glad you've come around. So allow me to give you a little tour. The side bar to the left of this box is where the main navigation panel for this website lies.

Projects

This tab is a branch of sorts. Hosting links to the various pages that each of my projects can be found on. My current projects that can be found here are:

  • DEGENERATIVE

    • ...An old and forgotten video game. DEGENERATIVE for the Doll OS..... An old school RPG about an office worker, a spirit medium, and a detective. Three unlikely souls, living in a world dominated by technology. A world where nature is drowned out. These three strangers are all that will stand between salvation, or total corruption.

  • WORLD OF FEAR

    • A sprawling connected universe of horror stories. Retellings of famous creepypasta stories, brand new stories from my own twisted mind, A world of monsters, horrors, and fear. A world where Slenderman, Jeff the Killer, and many others all exist at the same time.

Creepypasta Reviews (COMING SOON)

This page is home to my series known as "The Dollhouse View" from YouTube. In this series I review various creepypastas from around the internet. Going over what I like and dislike about them, and rating them on a scale of 25 points.

Horror Archives (COMING SOON)

This is a massive project of mine. Internet horror becomes lost media each and everyday. Creepypastas and creepy videos are deleted and vanish off the face of the earth. Never to be seen again. I despise when this happens. And will do my part to avoid it. The Dollhouse Archives are a large collection of creepypastas that I've personally saved from all across the web. I won't stop until I've saved as many as I possibly can.
