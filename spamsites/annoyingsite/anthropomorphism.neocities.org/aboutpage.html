What is Anthropomorphism?

While it was touched on briefly on the homepage, I didn't really explain it so I hope this will help.

Anthropomorphism is a word that is supposed to describe something that has humanistic qualities but is not human. It stems off the word anthropomorpic and anthropology, the study of anti-humanoid things. In this case the anthropomorphic creature is 00 (AiRA). Anthropomorphism or Anthro-tech is supposed to be the adventures/discovery of self conciousness, a way to find out how oneself is. While it is a goofy comic it does have some darker themes/meanings so just be aware.
