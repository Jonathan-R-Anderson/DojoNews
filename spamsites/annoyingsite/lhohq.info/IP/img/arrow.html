"My eyes seem blurred. This awful glare seems to have affected them. They'll be all right in a little while." Gloria looked at the dial and found that the hands pointed to four o'clock. They had been lost for six hours, but after their experiences, it seemed more like as many days. They rested a little while longer talking but little. "You carried me," said Gloria once.
