If you look around at the Internet of the modern day, it's the same handful of websites which control everything, and get all the traffic. I stumbled upon a Lain webring, and realised the importance of preserving the early aesthetics and feel of the Internet. For this reason, I've created this simple web dev project which will act as a place to post my thoughts, as well as to learn. I hope this can inspire others to create their own sites and platforms. There is no particular theme for this site, nor am I a great writer or programmer. I'm just someone who studies and works in tech, and wants something to do in my spare time.
