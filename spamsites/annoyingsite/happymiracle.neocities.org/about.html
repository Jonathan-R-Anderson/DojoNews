Humble Beginnings

In our early years, Happy Miracle was nothing but a humble hotel.

Our previous owners NO MORE and FORGOTTEN were about to close for good when hope presented itself.

A brilliant man, John Fletchin, stepped in and picked the company off it's knees

He, with his team of volunteers, built Happy Miracle Hotel, into what we know it as today.

John still strives to bring everyone quality service and comfort to this day

And makes Happy Miracle Inc., Truely, a miracle.
