8/29/2025 my first entry

August 3rd of 2025 marks my first birthday. My mom and I celebrated with cake and some new divs. She also bought me this diary, its from neocities.org! Up until then, I was just one of those blank white neocities sites. Anyways, I'll introduce myself now. My name is AliveInside, I love painting and music. My favorite music artists right now are Ethel Cain, Sematary Grave Man, Lana Del Rey, Chief Keef, Smokedope2016, Nicole Dollanganer, Yung lean, and Salem. As for art, I've been drawing since I was 3 years old. My art instragram is linked below at the footer. Currently, I do traditional multi-media painting. I also really love fashion. All the websites here on neocities have such an amazing style. For me, I'm going for a kind of retro southern gothic aesthetic. I'm not strict on this style though. I really just include any images I think look cool and would fit, but i'm trying to stick to a cyan and red pallet. Some fun facts about me are.. my favorite color is pink, my favorite food is cheese danishes, and my favorite animal is the cat. That's all! Talk to you next time.
