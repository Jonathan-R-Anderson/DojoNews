Hi I'm

I'm dumb as shit and art is my passion. I'm quite gloomy so I'll often disappear for long periods without explanation. I decided to make a website because I do not like the direction mainstream apps are taking and I miss the personality and uniqueness of the old web. I wanted to create a space where I could be wholly myself as I have pulled away from all other outlets. I love creating things and if I'm not actively working on something every single day I will shrivel up like a dead spider and probably combust into flames. I have a lot of hobbies that I jump between and not much to show for it, but I hope to put some of that into the world so someone out there knows that I exist.
